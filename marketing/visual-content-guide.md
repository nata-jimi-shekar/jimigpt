# Visual Content Guide
## What to Show People So They "See" JimiGPT

> Your Spanish teacher didn't get it from the description. She got it
> from the SCENARIO. Visual content bridges that gap — when people SEE
> a sample message on a phone screen, it clicks instantly.

---

## Message Card Templates

Create these in Canva (free tier). Each card is a single image showing
a pet message as it would appear on a phone screen.

### Card Format

```
┌─────────────────────────────┐
│                             │
│    🐾 [PET NAME]           │
│    [Archetype label]        │
│                             │
│  ┌───────────────────────┐  │
│  │                       │  │
│  │  [MESSAGE TEXT]       │  │
│  │                       │  │
│  └───────────────────────┘  │
│                             │
│    [Time stamp]             │
│                             │
│         jimigpt.com         │
└─────────────────────────────┘
```

### Color Per Archetype
- Chaos Gremlin: Bright orange/coral background
- Loyal Shadow: Warm golden/amber background
- Regal One: Cool silver/lavender background
- Gentle Soul: Soft sage/mint background
- Anxious Sweetheart: Pale pink background

### Cards to Create (Priority Order)

**Card Set 1: The Hook Cards (Create first — these go on the landing page)**

| Card | Archetype | Time | Message |
|------|-----------|------|---------|
| 1A | Chaos Gremlin | 5:58 PM | "FIVE MINUTES. I am being very patient. This is me being patient. ARE YOU COMING? 🐾" |
| 1B | Loyal Shadow | 2:30 PM | "Hey. I know you're busy. Just wanted you to know I'm on the couch, waiting. Take your time." |
| 1C | Regal One | 11:15 AM | "I've been staring out the window for 40 minutes. I've seen things you wouldn't understand." |

**Card Set 2: The Scenario Cards (For social media + conversations)**

| Card | Scenario | Archetype | Message |
|------|----------|-----------|---------|
| 2A | WFH Companion | Any | "You've been at that desk for 2 hours. Stretch break. I'll supervise." |
| 2B | Conflict Bridge | Loyal Shadow | "Hey, {name} gave me extra belly rubs today. I think they're feeling things. You know what to do. 🐾" |

**Card Set 3: The Arc Cards (Show multiple messages = a story)**

Create a multi-card carousel (Instagram) or a single tall image showing
the feeding anticipation arc:

```
🐾 MAX THE CHAOS GREMLIN

5:30 PM  "I can tell it's almost time. I'm
          positioning myself near the kitchen.
          Casually."

5:55 PM  "Five minutes. I'm counting. Don't
          think I can't tell time."

6:00 PM  "IT'S TIME. IT'S TIME. IT'S TIME. 🐾"

6:15 PM  "Hello?? Did you forget about me??
          I'm wasting away here."

                              jimigpt.com
```

This arc card is the MOST shareable format. It tells a complete micro-story.

## Phone Mockup Screenshots

**Even more powerful than cards:** Show messages as they'd appear in the
phone's actual SMS app. Use a phone mockup tool:

- **shotsnapp.com** (free) — paste text, get phone screenshot
- **mockuphone.com** (free) — similar
- Or just screenshot your OWN phone when self-dogfooding (Phase 1D)

The phone mockup makes it REAL. A designed card says "concept."
A phone screenshot says "this exists."

## Scenario Visuals (For Landing Page)

Each scenario section on the landing page has:
1. A short headline ("Your pet checks on you at work")
2. One sentence of context
3. One or two message cards showing the scenario
4. A small human icon showing the situation (remote worker at desk,
   parent with teenager, couple on couch)

Use simple illustrations, not photos. Photos of real people create
the wrong association. Illustrations keep it warm and universal.

**Free illustration sources:**
- undraw.co (customizable color illustrations)
- storyset.com (animated or static illustrations)
- humaaans.com (people illustrations, customizable)

## Content Creation Reps

**Rep 1: Design 3 message cards in Canva (Card Set 1)**
Time: 20 minutes. Use the template above. Keep it simple.
Export as PNG, 1080x1350 (Instagram portrait) and 1080x1080 (square).

**Rep 2: Create 1 arc card (Card Set 3)**
Time: 20 minutes. The feeding anticipation arc. Show 4 messages.
This is the piece that makes people say "I need to show this to someone."

**Rep 3: Create phone mockup of best message**
Time: 20 minutes. Take the best message from your parallel testing.
Put it in a phone mockup. This becomes the hero image for the landing page.

**Total: One M-CREATE container produces 5 visual assets.**
These assets are used across all channels: landing page, social media,
conversations (show on your phone), and outreach messages.
